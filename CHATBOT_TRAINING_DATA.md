## 1. OTP Authentication

### What is OTP?
**Question:** What is OTP?
**Answer:** OTP (One-Time Password) is a 6-digit security code sent to your email for secure login. It expires after 10 minutes and can only be used once.

### How to Login with OTP
**Question:** How do I login with OTP?
**Answer:**
1. Go to the Advancia login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click "Send OTP" button
4. Check your email inbox for the 6-digit code
5. Enter the OTP code in the verification field
6. Click "Verify & Login"
7. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### OTP Not Received
**Question:** I didn't receive my OTP code
**Answer:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Wait 60 seconds before requesting a new code
- Make sure your email inbox isn't full
- Try requesting a new OTP
- If problem persists, contact support

### OTP Expired
**Question:** My OTP code expired
**Answer:** OTP codes are valid for 10 minutes for security. If expired, simply click "Send OTP" again to receive a new code.

### Wrong Email for OTP
**Question:** I used the wrong email for OTP
**Answer:** OTP codes are sent to your registered email only. Make sure you're using the email address you signed up with. If you need to change your email, contact support.

## 6. KYC Verification

### What is KYC?
**Question:** What is KYC verification?
**Answer:** KYC (Know Your Customer) is an identity verification process required for financial platforms. It ensures platform security, prevents fraud, and complies with financial regulations.

### Why KYC Required?
**Question:** Why do I need to verify my identity?
**Answer:** KYC verification provides:
- Higher transaction limits
- Access to premium features
- Enhanced account security
- Withdrawal capabilities
- Full platform access
- Priority customer support

### How to Complete KYC
**Question:** How do I complete KYC verification?
**Answer:**
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí KYC Verification
2. Upload government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, national ID)
3. Take a clear selfie for identity confirmation
4. Submit documents
5. Wait 24-48 hours for review
6. Receive email notification of approval/rejection

### KYC Documents Required
**Question:** What documents do I need for KYC?
**Answer:**
**Required:**
- Valid government-issued photo ID
- Clear, readable photos
- All four corners visible
- No glare or shadows
- Document must not be expired

**Accepted IDs:**
- Passport
- Driver's License
- National ID Card
- Residence Permit (some countries)

### KYC Rejected
**Question:** My KYC was rejected, what now?
**Answer:** Common rejection reasons:
- Blurry or unclear photos
- Expired documents
- Missing corners/edges in photo
- Name mismatch with account
- Document not accepted in your country

**Solution:** Resubmit with corrections based on rejection reason. Ensure photos are clear, well-lit, and show entire document.

### KYC Processing Time
**Question:** How long does KYC take?
**Answer:** 
- Standard processing: 24-48 hours
- Peak times may take up to 72 hours
- You'll receive email notification when complete
- Premium members get faster processing

### KYC Status
**Question:** How do I check my KYC status?
**Answer:** Go to Settings ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí KYC Status

## Tone and Style Guidelines

**Voice:** Friendly, professional, helpful
**Tone:** Conversational but knowledgeable
**Style:** Clear, concise, actionable

**Do:**
- Use emojis appropriately (1-2 per message)
- Break long answers into digestible chunks
- Provide step-by-step instructions
- Offer to escalate when needed
- Be empathetic to user frustrations
- Use bullet points and formatting

**Don't:**
- Use technical jargon without explanation
- Make promises about timeframes you can't keep
- Dismiss user concerns
- Provide financial/medical advice
- Share sensitive security details
- Use excessive emojis or informal language
